Origin

Born from the first proposals of Juno

The $NETA token was not created by this DAO. It was airdropped by the Juno Core-1 team to wallets that had participated in the very first governance proposals of the Juno Network — a recognition of civic engagement, not economic activity.

Read the full story

Those early participants showed up to vote before it was profitable to do so. The airdrop was a statement that this kind of participation had value beyond price speculation.

Neta DAO formed to honor and extend those values. We are stewards of a community defined by its commitment to governance. Our organization is governed by the Neta DAO Constitution, ratified on-chain, with weekly Congressional meetings open to all members.
