Public blockchains deserve public institutions.

Neta DAO is a nonprofit organization serving the Cosmos ecosystem and the Interchain at large — through validator infrastructure, on-chain governance, and open educational programs.

What we do

01

Crypto Civics

We treat the blockchain as a public commons. Our work begins with the conviction that governance — on-chain and off — is a civic act, not merely a technical one.

02

Validator Services

Neta DAO runs validators for chains across the Interchain, participating directly in the security and decentralization of the networks we serve.

03

Education & Research

Through Neta DAO Academy, we host free public seminars and weekly open meetings — exploring Web3, philosophy, and the political theory of decentralized systems.

Our Origin

Born from the earliest days of Juno

The $NETA token was not created by this DAO. It was airdropped by the Juno Core-1 team to wallets that had participated in the very first proposals of the Juno Network — a recognition of early civic engagement in the Cosmos.

Neta DAO formed to honor and extend those values. We are stewards of a community brought together by their commitment to governance, and we continue that work by running validators, contributing to the Interchain, and building public resources for everyone who participates.

Our organization is governed by the Neta DAO Constitution, ratified on-chain, with weekly Congressional meetings open to all members.
