Neta DAO is a public institution — governed by its members, open to all. Here's how to participate.

01 — Governance

Stake $NETA

Staking $NETA in the DAO grants you voting rights on governance proposals. Every decision Neta DAO makes — from operations to constitutional changes — is decided by stakers.

Important

Staking with Neta DAO confers governance rights only — there are no yield rewards. Unbonding period: 91 days.

Congressional Meetings

Weekly Open Sessions

Neta DAO's weekly Congressional meetings are open to all members and the public. These are the legislative sessions of the DAO — where proposals are debated, operations reviewed, and direction set.

Meetings are announced on X and Discord. Recordings are published after each session.
