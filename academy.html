Neta DAO Academy

An educational initiative dedicated to thinking rigorously about the Interchain — through public seminars, open meetings, and eventually a peer-reviewed journal. Free. No prerequisites. Everyone welcome.

Programs

Open to everyone, always free

The Academy treats intellectual life as a public good. Our programs are designed to be accessible to anyone curious about Web3, governance, or the ideas shaping decentralized systems.

Public Seminars

Free seminars on Web3, blockchain governance, technology, and philosophy. Topics range from the practical mechanics of Cosmos to post-Lacanian theory applied to decentralized systems. Sessions are open to all and recorded when possible.

Weekly Congressional Meetings

The legislative sessions of Neta DAO — open to all members. Business, proposals, debates, and decisions happen here. These meetings are the DAO's primary deliberative forum and the closest thing to a town hall in the Interchain.

Research & Writing

The Academy produces written work on Interchain governance, Web3 political theory, and the philosophical questions raised by decentralized systems. This work feeds into the forthcoming Fork journal and is shared publicly.

Resources & Reading

Curated reading lists and resources for anyone seeking to understand Cosmos governance, crypto civics, and the theoretical frameworks that inform our work — from Lacan to Foucault to the Cosmos whitepaper.

Forthcoming

Fork: The Journal of Interchain Theory & Politics

Fork is a forthcoming digital journal dedicated to the theory and politics of decentralized systems. It will publish peer-reviewed work on Interchain governance, crypto civics, the philosophy of decentralization, and the political economy of Web3.

The journal takes its name from the blockchain concept of a fork — the moment when a shared history diverges and two possible futures emerge. We believe this is also a description of the political moment we are in.

Fork will be open access, community-governed, and eventually hosted at fork.netadao.org.
