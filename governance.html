Governance Proposals

Every decision Neta DAO makes is recorded on the Juno blockchain. Proposals below are loaded live from the DAO DAO indexer. Click any proposal to view the full text and vote record.

How governance works

Proposals require a $NETA deposit to submit. Voting is open to all staked $NETA holders for 5 days. A majority threshold with 10% quorum is required to pass.

Passed proposals are executed by the Executor subDAO after the voting period closes.
